Pancakes

Soft, fluffy round cakes made from flour, milk, and eggs, cooked on a pan. Commonly enjoyed with syrup, fruits, or butter for breakfast.

Cupcakes

Miniature cakes baked in cups, topped with colorful frosting or sprinkles. Perfect for desserts, birthdays, and celebrations.

Hummus

A creamy Middle Eastern dip made from chickpeas, tahini, lemon, and garlic. Served with pita or veggies, it's nutritious and vegan-friendly.

Hamburger

A sandwich made with a juicy beef patty in a bun, often with lettuce, cheese, and sauces. It's a classic fast-food favorite worldwide.

Salmon

A healthy, protein-rich fish often grilled, baked, or eaten raw in sushi. Known for its rich flavor and omega-3 fatty acids.

Vegetables

Edible plants like carrots, broccoli, and spinach, packed with nutrients and fiber. Essential for a balanced, healthy diet.
